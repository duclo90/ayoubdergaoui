Frequently asked questions

Can you become a web developer in 4 months?

Yes, it is possible to become a web developer, in 4 months, able to be hired as a junior at the end of your training at the 3W Academy. Therefore, the training program requires a lot of work and investment. Our learners do 50 to 60 hours of work per week, in the classroom and at home. So you're not going to become a web developer in 4 months if you code one hour a day!

What are your training opportunities ?

Computer development professions have something to seduce: they monopolize more than 13% of the average share of job offers in Morocco. The sector provides favorable employment conditions and integration into stable and lasting employment. In Casablanca alone, there is a need for 2,000 to 3,000 junior developers per year. Our winners find a job as a junior WEB developer in different structures: from local start-ups to large groups, including SMEs and digital agencies. They work as employees, freelancers or both at the same time!

i've never touched the code before, is this for me ?

Whether you have a background in web development or not, our training does not require any technical prerequisites. On the other hand, we allocate a week “Week 0” to logic tests and to the evaluation of your motivation and commitment to provide the effort required to successfully complete the training. Week 0 is an opportunity for you too to get a taste of our training method before starting the adventure together.

How is the assessment of learner's achievements carried out ?

Bootcamp mode allows daily evaluation through the day's deliverables. But all the same, there is a series of technical and Soft Skills evaluations that allow us to have a close idea of ​​the progress of each participant's learning.

What is the difference between telepresentiel training and traditional online training ?

Telepresential training has nothing to do with traditional online training such as MOOCs for example. The telepresential format ensures that the training takes place at a distance, but in permanent interactivity between the trainer and the learners. The courses are done live, the trainer exchanges with the learners and the learners exchange with each other. In other words, it is a face-to-face training, but in telework.

Why a bootcamp rather then a classic training ?

Classical training emphasizes the theoretical side and lasts much longer. A bootcamp, on the other hand, allows you to develop real-world skills and come out with a solid portfolio at the end of a course that lasts three to six months. Bootcamp training programs are purely practical and perfectly adapted to the needs of recruiters. Also, the trainers are professional developers who have above all practiced in business what they pass on to learners and use their operational experience to illustrate their course.
